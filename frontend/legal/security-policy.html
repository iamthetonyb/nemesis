Security Policy

We welcome good-faith vulnerability reports that improve the safety and reliability of USA Spending Watch.

Contact

Email security reports to security@usaspend.us. Include steps to reproduce, affected URL, browser, and impact.

Safe Harbor

If your research is conducted in good faith, avoids privacy harm, avoids service disruption, and gives us time to fix before public disclosure, we will not pursue legal action against you for that research.

Out of Scope

Do not test denial of service, social engineering, physical attacks, spam, or attacks against third-party providers. Do not access, alter, or retain data that is not yours.

Response

We aim to acknowledge reports within 7 days and prioritize fixes based on exploitability, user impact, and deployment risk.
