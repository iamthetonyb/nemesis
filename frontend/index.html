How it works

USA Spending Watch · Open-source citizen audit
🔍

What is this?

An open-source tool that surfaces government spending data by county, agency, and source type. Every figure links to an official record, including USAspending.gov, state checkbooks, and county portals.

🗺

Using the map

  • Click any county to open its spending records
  • Use filter chips (Federal / State / Local / District) to narrow by source
  • Switch to State view for Nevada-level aggregates
  • Search the sidebar to find a specific jurisdiction or agency
📈

What do the tiers mean?

Low / Medium / High / Extreme Volume are amount-volume signals — not fraud findings. They indicate how large a record is relative to others. High volume = worth reviewing. It is not an allegation of wrongdoing.

📄

Data sources

  • Federal: USAspending.gov FY 2026 awards & obligations
  • Nevada State: OpenGov state checkbook (~$960M)
  • Clark County School District: OpenGov portal
  • Counties: Nevada county ACFR financial reports
  • Geography: TIGERweb Census boundaries
🔗
